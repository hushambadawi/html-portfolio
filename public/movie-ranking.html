My top 3 movies of all time

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful story of hope and friendship as a wrongly convicted man uses his resilience and wits to survive decades in prison.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending heist film where dreams and reality blur as a team of specialists attempt to plant an idea in a target's subconscious.

The Godfather (1972)

A gripping tale of power, loyalty, and betrayal within a crime family navigating the perilous world of the mafia.
